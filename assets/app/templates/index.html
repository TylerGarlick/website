RedRocket Persistence

The RedRocket framework was conceptualized about 5 years ago but didn't officially materialize into a framework until about 2 years ago. The team I was working on needed a framework to abstract away it's entities to minimize friction when testing. The framework did just that an allow the team to build applications built on concepts of inversion of control and test driven development. The framework is built in C# and targets Entity Framework, and RavenDB.
